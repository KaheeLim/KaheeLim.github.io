About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Electrical Engineering at KAIST, advised by Prof. Steven Euijong Whang. My research focuses on responsible AI and trustworthy large language models, with an emphasis on fairness, hidden bias, response consistency, and sycophancy in personalized LLMs.

I am interested in understanding when and why large language models behave unfairly or unreliably in real-world user interactions. My recent work studies hidden bias caused by prompt framing and sycophantic behavior in personalized LLM settings.
